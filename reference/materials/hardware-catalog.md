# Hardware Catalog

Reference for commonly used cabinet hardware. Update with specific brands/suppliers as you establish preferences.

## Hinges

### Concealed (European) Hinges
| Type | Use | Overlay | Notes |
|------|-----|---------|-------|
| Full overlay | Covers entire face frame stile | 1/2" - 3/4" | Most common |
| Half overlay | Shares stile with adjacent door | ~1/4" | At pairs of doors |
| Inset | Door sits flush with face frame | 0" | Tighter tolerances required |

**Features to specify:**
- Soft-close: Yes (standard for client work)
- Opening angle: 110° (standard), 170° (for full access)
- Mounting: Face frame or frameless (Euro)
- Cup size: 35mm (standard)

### Butt Hinges
For face-frame inset doors. Available in various finishes.

## Drawer Slides

| Type | Extension | Load Rating | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Side-mount, full extension | 100% | 75-100 lbs | Most common, requires 1/2" clearance per side |
| Under-mount, full extension | 100% | 75 lbs | Hidden, premium look, soft-close built in |
| Center-mount | 75% | 35 lbs | Budget option, less stable |

**Common lengths:** 12", 14", 16", 18", 20", 22", 24"

**Always specify soft-close** for client work.

## Pulls and Knobs
Measured center-to-center (boring):
- **Knobs:** Single hole, 1" - 1-1/2" diameter
- **Pulls:** Common borings: 3", 3-3/4", 5", 6-5/16", 7-9/16", 10", 12"
- **General rule:** Knobs on doors, pulls on drawers (but client preference rules)

## Shelf Supports
| Type | Hole Size | Notes |
|------|-----------|-------|
| Spoon-style pins | 1/4" | Most common, easy to install |
| Plug-in pins | 5mm | European standard |
| Shelf pin with rubber bumper | 1/4" | Prevents shelf sliding |

**Drilling jig:** Use a shelf pin drilling jig for consistent 32mm spacing.
