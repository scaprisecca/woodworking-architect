## Quote Validity
- Quotes valid for **30 days** from date issued
- After 30 days, material prices may need to be re-checked

## Change Orders
- Changes after design approval: Bill at $___/hr for redesign time + material cost difference
- Always get change orders in writing before proceeding

## Contingency
- Add ___% contingency to material estimates for:
  - Cutting errors
  - Material defects
  - Unforeseen site conditions
  - Typically 10-15%

## Discounts
- Repeat customers: ___% discount
- Referral credit: $___
- Multi-room projects: ___% discount on additional rooms

## What to Include in Every Quote
1. Detailed scope of work
2. Materials specified (species, grade, finish)
3. Hardware specified (brand/model or equivalent)
4. What's NOT included (countertops, plumbing, electrical, painting walls, etc.)
5. Timeline estimate
6. Payment schedule
7. Change order policy
8. Warranty terms

## Warranty
- **Workmanship:** ___ year(s)
- **Covers:** Defects in construction, finish failure under normal use
- **Does not cover:** Normal wear, client modifications, water damage from leaks, abuse
