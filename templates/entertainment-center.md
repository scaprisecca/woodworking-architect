# Entertainment Center / Media Wall — Project Template

## Common Configurations

### Built-in Wall Unit
Full wall or partial wall unit with TV mounting area, shelving, and base storage.
- Typical width: 8'-16' (spans full wall or fireplace wall)
- Typical height: 7'-8' (floor to ceiling or with crown molding)
- Typical depth: 16"-24" (shallower than kitchen cabinets)

### Floating Media Console
Wall-mounted console below TV with concealed storage.
- Typical width: 5'-8'
- Typical height: 12"-20"
- Typical depth: 14"-18"

### Fireplace Surround with Built-ins
Flanking cabinets/shelves on either side of a fireplace.
- Tower width: 18"-30" each side
- Mantel integration considerations

### TV Frame / Niche
Recessed area for TV with surrounding trim and shelving.

## Typical Materials
- **Carcass:** 3/4" plywood (paint-grade birch or hardwood if stained)
- **Backs:** 1/4" plywood (can use 1/2" for structural if wall-mounted)
- **Shelves:** 3/4" plywood, may need face-glued hardwood nosing
- **Face frames:** 3/4" x 1-1/2" hardwood (if face-frame style)
- **Doors:** 3/4" plywood slab, or frame-and-panel
- **Crown/base molding:** Hardwood to match or paint-grade
- **Adjustable shelf pins:** 1/4" spoon-style or plug-in

## Special Considerations
- **Ventilation:** Receiver/components generate heat. Plan for airflow (back panel gaps, ventilated shelves, or small fans)
- **Wire management:** Route cables behind the unit. Consider a cable chase or conduit. Grommets in shelves for vertical runs
- **Weight:** TVs 55"+ can exceed 40lbs. Wall-mounted units need proper blocking. Free-standing units need adequate shelf support spans
- **Shelf span:** For 3/4" plywood shelves, max unsupported span ~30" to prevent sag. Add a center divider or use 1" material for wider spans
- **Lighting wiring:** Plan wire routes before assembly. LED strip channels should be routed into dados
- **Symmetry:** Entertainment centers are focal points — asymmetry is very noticeable. Double-check that left and right sides mirror correctly

## Common Upgrades to Offer
- LED accent lighting (in-cabinet or under-shelf)
- Soft-close hinges and drawer slides
- Glass doors with adjustable shelves for display sections
- Pull-out component shelf for easy access to receiver
- Hidden compartment for router/modem
- Integrated power strip with surge protection
- Touch-latch doors (push-to-open, no visible hardware)
- Book-matched veneer panels for a premium look
