# General Woodworking — Project Template

## This Covers
Any custom woodworking project that isn't a cabinet, closet, or built-in:
- Tables (dining, coffee, end, console)
- Desks and workstations
- Benches (indoor, outdoor, entryway)
- Bed frames and headboards
- Mantels and fireplace surrounds
- Wainscoting and wall panels
- Custom trim and molding
- Picture frames
- Outdoor furniture
- Pet furniture
- Kids' furniture

## Consultation Questions

- [ ] What is being built?
- [ ] What will it be used for?
- [ ] Indoor or outdoor use?
- [ ] Any weight it needs to support?
- [ ] Desired dimensions?
- [ ] Style (modern, rustic, traditional, mid-century, industrial)?

### Materials
- [ ] Wood species preference?

### Environment
- [ ] Where will this live? (room, outdoor, garage)
- [ ] Humidity/weather exposure?
- [ ] Pets or children to consider?
- [ ] Floor type where it will sit?

## Measurements
- [ ] Available space (width, depth, height)
- [ ] Clearances needed (walkways, doors)
- [ ] Matching existing furniture dimensions?
- [ ] Ergonomic heights (desk: 28"-30", dining: 30", counter: 36", bar: 42")

## Special Considerations
- **Wood movement:** Solid wood expands/contracts with humidity. Plan for it in wide panels (breadboard ends, floating panels, expansion gaps)
- **Joinery selection:** Match joinery to the project's structural and aesthetic needs
- **Outdoor use:** Requires weather-resistant species and marine-grade finish or exterior-rated hardware
- **Finish selection:** Must match the project's use case (food-safe for cutting boards/tables, waterproof for outdoor, etc.)
