# Bookcase / Built-in Shelving — Project Template

## Common Configurations

### Floor-to-Ceiling Built-in
Full wall of shelving, possibly flanking a window or doorway.

### Freestanding Bookcase
Standalone unit, may need to be transportable.

### Window Seat with Shelving
Combination bench/storage with flanking bookshelves.

## Typical Materials
- **Carcass:** 3/4" plywood
- **Shelves:** 3/4" plywood (3/4" hardwood for spans over 30")
- **Back:** 1/4" plywood or beadboard
- **Face frame:** 3/4" hardwood
- **Trim:** Match existing room trim

## Special Considerations
- **Shelf sag:** 3/4" plywood max span ~30" without support. Add hardwood nosing or use 1" stock for wider spans
- **Weight:** Books are heavy (~20 lbs/linear foot). Ensure shelf pins and supports are rated
- **Anti-tip:** Tall freestanding units MUST be secured to wall
- **Scribing:** Built-ins need scribe strips to fit against uneven walls/ceilings

## Common Upgrades to Offer
- LED strip lighting (warm white, 2700K)
- Adjustable shelf pin system with extra holes
- Glass shelves for display sections
- Ladder rail (for tall units)
- Hidden storage compartments
- Cable management grommets
