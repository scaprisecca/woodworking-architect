# Kitchen Cabinets — Project Template

## Common Configurations

### Full Kitchen Remodel
Complete cabinet replacement — base, wall, and tall/pantry units.

### Partial Update
Replace specific sections (e.g., new island, replace base cabinets only).

### Island / Peninsula
Standalone or attached unit with storage and possibly seating.

## Project-Specific Consultation Questions

### Layout and Usage
- [ ] How many people cook at the same time?
- [ ] Right-handed or left-handed primary cook?
- [ ] Current pain points with the kitchen layout?
- [ ] Do they entertain? How many guests typically?
- [ ] Do they need a breakfast bar or seating at the island?

### Appliances
- [ ] Refrigerator: size, type (French door, side-by-side), depth (standard vs counter-depth)?
- [ ] Range/cooktop: width (30" or 36"), gas or electric, slide-in or freestanding?
- [ ] Dishwasher: standard 24" or compact?
- [ ] Microwave: over-range, built-in, or countertop (needs shelf)?
- [ ] Vent hood: type and size?
- [ ] Any specialty appliances? (wine fridge, double oven, warming drawer)
- [ ] Existing appliances staying or all new?

### Storage Needs
- [ ] How much pantry storage needed?
- [ ] Baking supplies storage?
- [ ] Small appliance garage needed? (mixer, toaster, blender)
- [ ] Spice storage preference?
- [ ] Pot and pan storage preference?
- [ ] Trash and recycling: how many bins, what size?
- [ ] Under-sink organization?

### Style
- [ ] Door style (shaker, slab, raised panel, beadboard)?
- [ ] Paint or stain?
- [ ] Two-tone (different color upper/lower)?
- [ ] Countertop material (affects overhang, edge detail, support)?
- [ ] Backsplash plans (affects where wall cabinets sit)?
- [ ] Hardware style and size?

## Measurements Specific to Kitchens

### Critical Dimensions
- [ ] Each wall length (inside corner to inside corner)
- [ ] Distance between walls at multiple heights
- [ ] Window sill height and window width (each window)
- [ ] Distance from corner to window (each side)
- [ ] Soffit depth, height from floor, and distance from wall
- [ ] Ceiling height at all corners and center

### Plumbing
- [ ] Sink drain location (center of drain from left wall and back wall)
- [ ] Water supply locations
- [ ] Dishwasher water/drain location
- [ ] Gas line location (if gas range)
- [ ] Ice maker water line location

### Electrical
- [ ] All outlet locations and heights
- [ ] Switch locations
- [ ] Dedicated circuits for appliances
- [ ] Under-cabinet lighting preference

### Work Triangle
- [ ] Current sink-to-stove distance
- [ ] Current stove-to-fridge distance
- [ ] Current sink-to-fridge distance

## Typical Materials
- **Carcass:** 3/4" plywood (birch or maple for paint-grade)
- **Backs:** 1/4" plywood
- **Shelves:** 3/4" plywood with edge banding
- **Face frames:** 3/4" x 1-1/2" hardwood
- **Doors:** Plywood slab or frame-and-panel
- **Drawer boxes:** 1/2" plywood or hardwood
- **Toe kick:** 1/4" plywood or matching laminate

## Special Considerations
- **Work triangle:** Sink-stove-fridge triangle perimeter should be 12'-26' total
- **Counter space:** Minimum 15" on each side of cooktop, 24" on one side of sink
- **Clearances:** 36" minimum between opposing cabinets/islands for walkway, 42" preferred
- **Plumbing access:** Plan for shut-off valve access under sink
- **Fillers:** Plan for 3" filler strips at wall-to-cabinet transitions
- **Crown molding:** If ceiling isn't level, scribe molding or use light rail to hide gap

## Common Upgrades to Offer
- Soft-close hinges and full-extension drawer slides
- Pull-out trash/recycling cabinet
- Lazy susan corner cabinet
- Pull-out spice rack
- Appliance garage with tambour door
- Drawer organizers (utensils, knives, spices)
- Under-cabinet LED lighting
- Glass-front display cabinets
- Deep drawers for pots/pans instead of base cabinets
