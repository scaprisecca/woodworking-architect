# Bathroom Cabinets — Project Template

## Common Configurations

### Vanity Cabinet
Single or double sink vanity with storage below and optional tower.

### Linen Tower / Cabinet
Tall storage unit for towels, toiletries, cleaning supplies.

### Medicine Cabinet (Built-in)
Recessed or surface-mount cabinet above vanity.

## Typical Materials
- **Carcass:** 3/4" plywood (moisture-resistant grade)
- **Shelves:** 3/4" plywood with edge banding
- **Back:** 1/4" plywood
- **Doors/drawers:** Plywood with water-resistant finish
- **Finish:** Must be waterproof (polyurethane, conversion varnish, or paint with primer)

## Special Considerations
- **Moisture:** Bathrooms are high-humidity. Use moisture-resistant plywood, waterproof finish, and consider melamine interior
- **Plumbing access:** Plan for easy access to shut-offs and P-trap
- **Vanity height:** Standard 32"-34", comfort height 36" (match counter height to user preference)
- **Ventilation:** Ensure cabinet doesn't block bath fan or HVAC
- **Floating vanity:** Requires proper wall blocking; plan for concealed mounting bracket

## Common Upgrades to Offer
- Soft-close hinges and slides
- Pull-out waste basket
- Built-in electrical outlet inside drawer (for hair dryer, electric toothbrush)
- Tip-out tray at sink front
- Adjustable interior shelves
- Towel bar on cabinet side
