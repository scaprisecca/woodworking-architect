# Shelving — Project Template

## Common Configurations

### Floating Shelves
Wall-mounted shelves with concealed brackets/hardware.

### Bracket Shelves
Open shelving with visible decorative brackets.

### Utility Shelving
Garage, pantry, or closet shelving for storage (function over form).

### Display Shelves
Picture ledges, gallery walls, or decorative display surfaces.

## Typical Materials
- **Floating shelves:** 3/4" - 1-1/2" hardwood or hollow box from plywood
- **Bracket shelves:** 3/4" hardwood or plywood with nosing
- **Utility:** 3/4" plywood or melamine

## Special Considerations
- **Floating shelf internals:** Steel rod or French cleat hidden inside hollow box
- **Weight capacity:** Depends on mounting method, stud spacing, and span
- **Level:** Shelves at eye level — even 1/16" off-level is noticeable
- **Stud mounting:** Required for anything over 15 lbs; toggle bolts for between studs (limited weight)

## Common Upgrades to Offer
- Under-shelf LED lighting
- Integrated hooks (for mugs, utensils in kitchen)
- Custom edge profiles
- Live-edge slab shelves
- Hidden mounting hardware
